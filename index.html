Language learning

When we think about language learning what comes to our mind first is learnig a lot of grammar rules and solve a lot of problems. After doing all this, we take few exams and CONGRATULATIONS!!You have learned the language!

But languagre learning is not all about grammar and exam. A lot of people don't feel motivated because they use these conventional methods and stop midway.

Language learning is very interesting and fun if it is learned the right way. Before that I want to say that a lot of people learn the language which is selected by their institution but a person should choose his or her language.

Here is a list of some languages which most of the people learn in educational institutions in conventional ways and loose their motivation:
  • ENGLISH
  • SPANISH
  • FRENCH
  • GERMAN
This doesn't mean that people don't enjoy learning these. I will talk about it later.

Learning languages should be fun and it's not just about languages. We should learn everything in a fun way.
I want to tell you about one type of people who can speak different languages. They are POLYGLOTS. Most of the polyglots learn languages as hobbies and they enjoy learning languages very much. There's a misconception about the characteristics of a polyglot. Maybe you speak 4 languages and considered as a polyglot. On the other hand, you may speak 50 languages and not considered as a polyglot. Just learning a few lines of a language doesn't make someone a polyglot. You have to be able to communicate with people in your target language. There are 3 types of polyglot.
  1. Basic level fluent
  2. Conversation level fluent
  3. Native level fluent
