Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to learn a language?

There is nothing we can say about how long it takes. It is dependent on motivation, resources, money, environment, difficulty level, learner's own language etc

Can I learn languages?

YES. Anyone can learn languages if they give it a try and stay consistent. It's quite hard to learn a language without patience.

Is learning Arabic difficult?

Arabic might seem difficult at first because of the curly structure of the letters, different types of pronouns, bunch of verb conjugations but it gets easier later and it's a very unique language.
